THE DARK RIDER

When a mysterious video game shows up in John's mail, he is disappointed to find that it's a high fantasy role playing game. With nothing else to do, he plays it anyway, getting transported into the game. Having become a mage, John uses his new found powers to fight monsters, and finds out there's more to the game of Ilia than meets the eye.

To remain standing, the city will need to come to terms with its own dark secrets, or forever be lost in the blackest night.

THE SILVER BLADE

The Silver Blade, with eyes like the frost under moonlight and hair as pale as winter's first snow, stands as a sentinel against the darkness. His tale is woven from the threads of lost legends and whispered secrets, a saga that echoes through the ages. Once, he was the heart of a brotherhood sworn to defend the realm from specters unseen. Clad in night's own cloak, his sword was the only glimmer in the abyss, its azure glow a beacon of hope. When his brothers fell like stars plucked from the sky, he alone remained, a lone light in the encroaching shadow. Wearied by battle but unyielding, he wandered the wilds, a ghost among the trees. Each town he passed through whispered of the Silver Blade, a myth to scare children and inspire warriors. He was a legend living, but he bore the weight of his failures like chains. Fate, with her unfathomable eye, led him to a village besieged by nightmares made flesh. Here, his blade sang its deadly song once more, cleaving darkness from the night. It was a massacre, but amidst the chaos, he found purpose, and for a fleeting moment, he was the hero he once promised to be. As the dawn broke and the last demon fell, he found himself before a dying girl. Her eyes held the same blue as his blade, a mirror to his soul. In her final breath, she named him hero, and in her gaze, he found redemption. With each step forward, the Silver Blade carves his story into the bones of the earth. He is the whisper of hope when all light seems lost, the shield against the night. His saga continues, written in the stars and sung by the winds. A protector. A guardian. A hero reborn.

THE DARK RIDER

Have you ever heard of the “Rhyme of the Dark Rider?” It’s a lullaby recited by parents within the region of Victor’s Reign to scare their children into being good. Otherwise, Acanthus Asphodel would appear on his horse and take them away. Recently, high ranking officials within the Holy City of Haydenbough have begun to go missing and hushed whispers have spoken of sightings of a man in black armor, believed similar to that worn by the Dark Rider.

To remain standing, the city will need to come to terms with its own dark secrets, or forever be lost in the blackest night.
